# App Description · English

> For en-US, en-GB, etc. Maximum 4000 characters.

## Short pitch (first 4 lines)

DaysHere is a tiny macOS menu bar tool that counts the days you've actually been somewhere.

Whether you need a tax residency certificate that requires 183 calendar days per year, want to know how many weekends you actually spent at home, or simply want to look back at a year of where you lived — DaysHere turns it into one color block in your menu bar.

## Highlights

- **Single glance from the menu bar** — your current "days here" count and progress against 183 / 124 are right in the menu bar title
- **Multiple location profiles** — Hengqin, your hometown, a remote workspace abroad — each location keeps its own dataset
- **Year heatmap + month calendar** — same data, two views. Green = GPS-detected, dark green = manually marked, blue = leave bridge, yellow = holiday bridge
- **Smart holiday bridging** — Chinese 2026 public holidays built in. If you were there before and after a holiday, the whole holiday counts automatically.
- **iCloud sync across your Macs** — uses Apple Key-Value Storage. Sign in with the same Apple ID and your data merges automatically. No accounts, no servers.
- **JSON import / export** — fully compatible with the Chrome extension format. Move data between devices and tools without friction.
- **Local first, zero tracking** — everything lives on your Mac and your iCloud. No third-party servers, no analytics, no ads, ever.

## Who it's for

- People navigating residency-based tax / subsidy rules (e.g. Hengqin Guangdong-Macao 183-day / 124-workday rule)
- Remote workers tracking time across cities or countries
- Frequent travelers curious about where their year actually went
- Anyone who wants a Strava-grade heatmap, but for places they sleep

## Design principles

- **Quiet** — a single 24-pt cell in the menu bar; the full panel is one click away
- **Permission-respectful** — Location is only requested the moment you tap "Use current location"
- **Free** — core features are completely free with no in-app purchases

## Privacy

- No personal data is collected
- Location is used only on-device; coordinates never leave your Mac
- Cross-device sync uses Apple iCloud Key-Value Storage — data is encrypted and lives in your own iCloud
- No proprietary servers, no third-party analytics SDKs
